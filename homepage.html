About Us

Juices & Smoothie Co. is a vibrant franchise dedicated to crafting fresh, nutritious blends of fruits and vegetables. With locations nestled in the bustling cities of Boston, New York, and Washington D.C., our mission is to provide health-conscious individuals with convenient access to delicious and revitalizing beverages. From energizing smoothie bowls to refreshing cold-pressed juices, each creation is carefully curated to fuel and nourish our customers' active lifestyles. Join us in sipping towards wellness and vitality at Juices & Smoothie Co.
